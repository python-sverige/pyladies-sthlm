About

Our mission is to promote, educate and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events, and social gatherings.

PyLadies also aims to provide a friendly support network for women and a bridge to the larger Python world. Anyone with an interest in Python is encouraged to participate! Welcome!
